Cream Chikcen

It takes 5 minutes to prepare and 1 hour and 30 minutes to cook.

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1 pinch poultry seasoning
  • 1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup
  • 10 1/2 ounces milk

Steps To Take

  1. Preheat the over to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Place chicken in a 9x13-inch baking dish; season with poultry seasoning.
  3. Mix together condensed soup and milk in a bowl.
  4. Pour soup mixture evenly over chicken.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until chicken is no longer pink in the center and juices run clear, about 1 1/2 hours.
  6. An instant-read the thermometer inserted in the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C)
