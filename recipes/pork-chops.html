Smoked Pork Chops

"The prep time is 10 mins and the cooking time is 2 hours and 50 mins"

  • 1.5 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1.5 teaspoons light brown sugar
  • 1.5 teaspoons ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 0.5 teaspoon ground mustard
  • 0.5 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 0.5 teaspoon onion powder
  • 0.5 teaspoon oregano
  • 6 bone-in center-cut pork chops(about 1-inch thick)

"3 Steps To Make"

  1. Preheat the smoker to 250 degrees F (120 degrees C). Add wood chips according to manufacturer's directions.
  2. Combine salt, brown sugar, pepper, paprika, ground mustard, garlic powder, onion powder, and organo in a small bowl.
  3. Stir until evenly combined. Sprinkle over both sides of each pork chop
  4. Place pork chops directly on the smoker grate. Smoke for 2 hours 30 minutes.
  5. Remove chops from the smoker and let rest for 10 minutes.
